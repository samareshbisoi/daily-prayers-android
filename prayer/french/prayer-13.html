God Loves You

Prayer for overcoming fear


Loving Heavenly Father, You Holy Name is a strong tower. I run to You and I am safe. You are my Lord and my God. You are Emmanuel, God with us. The one Who is in me is greater than the one who is in the world. I am not afraid.

Lord, I am Your child and I am led by the Holy Spirit. I have nothing to fear. The Holy Spirit joins with my spirit to affirm that I am Your child.

Lord, You are in charge and in control of everything. I have nothing to fear as You are my Abba, Father.

Thank You, Father Lord. Please continue to bless me with Your peace. And Father, I pray for others who are also in need of Your mercy and grace in overcoming fears. Father, I pray in the mighty Name of Jesus. Amen.

Scripture Reference (Proverbs 18:10, 1 John 4:4, Romans 8: 14-16)
